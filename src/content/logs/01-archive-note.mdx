This log is a working index, not a promise of publication dates or final titles. Entries may be revised, merged, or removed as the manuscript tightens.

Use the frontmatter fields consistently:

- `image` should point to a file in `public/` (for example `/sketches/your-file.png`).
- `imageAlt` should describe the sketch for screen readers.

The body can hold notes, fragments, or research reminders—whatever keeps the work moving without pretending it is finished copy.
